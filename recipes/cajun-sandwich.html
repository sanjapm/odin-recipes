Cajun Egg Sandwich

Description

The perfect fried egg sandwich with just the right amount of spice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice Cheddar cheese
  • Mayonnaise to taste(or 1 teaspoon)
  • Mustard to taste(or 1 teaspoon)
  • Ketchup to taste(or 1 teaspoon)
  • Cajun seasoning to taste(or 1 teaspoon)
  • 1 dash hot pepper sauce
  • 2 slices white bread, toasted
  • 1 lettuce lead
  • 1 slice tomato

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-low heat
  2. Crack egg into the skillet and cook until egg is white on the bottom layer and is firm enough to flip, 2 to 3 minutes
  3. Flip egg, trying not to crack the yolk
  4. Place Cheddar cheese on top of egg and cook until cheese is melted and egg is cooked to desired doneness, 2 to 5 minutes
  5. Spread mayonnaise, mustard, and ketchup into both bread slices and season with Cajun seasoning and hot pepper sauce. Add lettuce and tomato to 1 bread slice; top with egg-cheese. Sprinkle more Cajun seasoning on egg
  6. Pour any remaining butter from skillet over egg and top with second piece of bread, creating a sandwich
